Aleppo

This ancient, industrious city has seen destruction from civil war fighting and an earthquake in 2023. Refugees poured out of the city. This map showcases photographs from across the area. Included are images of pre-destruction, post-destruction, and reconstruction efforts.

Concentration of Attention

Most of the international attention is focused on the famous Old City. This can be seen by the majority of markers placed in the middle of the city. Although important, this detracts from the crisis that returning refugees face in the rest of the city.
